FAQ

What is it like to work with Yolu?

We answer the most common questions about rights, geos and the required volume of content.

What type of content do you work with? +
Any catalog that complies with platform rules: films, series, TV shows, kids content and more. We review the library, highlight suitable titles and advise what cannot be published.
We have territorial restrictions. Is that a problem? +
No. We configure geoblocking and publish only in the countries for which you hold the rights.
We don’t have time to learn all the details. +
That’s exactly why our model exists. You transfer the library to us, we handle operations, and you receive additional income.
How do you earn money? +
We work on a revenue-share basis: we keep a percentage of the monetization that your content generates. Our incentives are fully aligned with yours.
What volume of rights is needed to start? +
There is no minimum threshold, but the largest catalogs generate the most revenue. Contact us, and we will advise you on the most effective monetization strategy for your content.
